Conducted a portfolio and investment analysis of two publicly traded companies — LVMH Moet Hennessy (Euronext: MC.PA) and Tapestry, Inc. (NYSE: TPR) — using weekly adjusted closing price data sourced from Yahoo Finance (53 observations, March 2025–March 2026). Built a structured Excel model across four analytical sheets covering raw data preparation, weekly return calculations, risk metrics, and data visualization. Applied quantitative methods including period-on-period return computation, standard deviation and variance analysis, Sharpe Ratio calculation (risk-free rate: 4.5% p.a.), and Pearson correlation to assess diversification benefits of a 50/50 equally-weighted portfolio. Results were interpreted in the context of Modern Portfolio Theory (Markowitz) and core finance principles. Demonstrated proficiency in Excel formula construction (AVERAGE, STDEV, VAR, CORREL, MIN, MAX), financial data sourcing, and the communication of analytical findings in a structured, professional format.
